BIO

Ruth O. Saxton is a professor of English and cofounder of the women's studies program at Mills College in Oakland, CA, where she has been recognized for exemplary teaching. She coedited Woolf and Lessing: Breaking the Mold (with Jean Tobin), edited The Girl, Constructions of the Girl in Contemporary Fiction by Women, and has recently finished a collection of essays about the aging female protagonist in contemporary fiction.
